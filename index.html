About the Founder

Josh Galloway, MS

Principal Data Science Consultant & Founder

Josh brings hands-on experience delivering industrial AI and engineering solutions that generate measurable business impact. His career spans roles as Principal Technical Expert at Schneider Electric's AI Hub, Data Scientist at Accenture's Applied Intelligence group, and Senior Advanced Process Control Engineer at multiple industrial operations including Koch Industries, Georgia-Pacific, and AkzoNobel.

At Schneider Electric, Josh developed a comprehensive strategic roadmap for AI-enhanced Advanced Process Control, defining how AI transforms traditional APC through five key enhancement areas: multi-objective optimization, predictive maintenance integration, anomaly detection, adaptive control strategy selection, and LLM-based explainability. These and other frameworks supported opportunities ranging from $200K to over $1M across desalination, chemicals, oil & gas, and pulp & paper operations.

Notable achievements include architecting a three-layer AI optimization system for polymer grade transitions, optimizing large-scale desalination operations (achieving 5-7% chemical usage reduction through ensemble ML combining Bayesian GMM and custom Temporal Deep CNN), and leading a cross-functional team leveraging GPT-3 for regulatory compliance document classification at a major California utility.

Josh's unique strength lies in bridging traditional engineering methodologies (Model Predictive Control, digital twins, first-principles modeling) with cutting-edge AI capabilities (LLMs, deep learning, reinforcement learning, physics-informed ML). This combination enables practical AI solutions grounded in real-world industrial constraints, delivering proven results: $5M+ in documented savings across energy savings, production optimization, and quality improvements.
